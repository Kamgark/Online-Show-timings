About us

The most amazing Movie experience in Lahore at the latest 3D THE Cinema, we have topped the line Sound Processor. The DCP 300 which has digital input for 16 channels Booth Monitor & Digital Crossover along with network control and monitoring of DSP, amplifiers & speakers in one integrated package which provides magical sound.

AUDIPLEX – I: is the newly commissioned luxury mini plex at Pakistan’s premier golf resort Royal Palm Golf & Country Club, Lahore is a state-of-the-art facility created for a unique movie entertainment experience which can also be used for viewing documentaries, live concerts and live telecast of international award ceremonies. This first of its kind facility in Pakistan can also live broadcast Global Sports events including Golf, Soccer, Formula One, Cricket, Tennis etc. In addition to playing popular blockbusters of Hollywood and Bollywood movies, AUDIPLEX is also an ideal venue for holding seminars, live webinars, and online board meetings. Next to AUDIPLEX there is another 150 seater conference and seminar room called “The Congress Room”#8221; and a smaller 10-40 seater room called “The Congress Board Room”. Both these rooms are equipped with Wi-Fi, in built Audio system, in built overhead projectors, roll up screens and fully customized to hold seminars, webinars, lectures, board presentations, product launches, press conferences, networking and PR duos.
